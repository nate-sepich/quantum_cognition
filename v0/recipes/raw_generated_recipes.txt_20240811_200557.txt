## 5 High-Protein, High-Carb Recipes (Under 30 Minutes, Using Your Inventory)

These recipes aim to hit your macro goals of {'protein': 190, 'carbs': 477, 'fat': 126} approximately, but may require slight adjustments based on specific ingredient brands and portion sizes. 

**Recipe 1: Chicken & Broccoli Fried Rice (Asian-Inspired)**

* **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
* **Additional Ingredients:** Soy sauce (low sodium), honey, garlic, ginger, frozen peas and carrots

**Instructions:**

1. **Cook Rice:** Prepare 2 cups of rice according to package instructions. 
2. **Prep Ingredients:** Dice chicken breasts, chop broccoli, mince garlic and ginger. 
3. **Stir-Fry:** Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan or wok. Stir-fry garlic & ginger for 30 seconds, then add chicken and cook until browned.
4. **Combine:** Add broccoli, peas & carrots, and cooked rice to the pan. Stir-fry for 5 minutes.
5. **Sauce:** In a small bowl, whisk together 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp honey, and 1 tbsp water. Pour over the rice mixture and stir until heated through.
6. **Serve:** Garnish with sesame seeds (optional) and enjoy!

**Recipe 2: Chicken Burrito Bowls with Cilantro-Lime Rice**

* **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
* **Additional Ingredients:** Canned black beans, salsa, avocado, lime, cilantro, cumin, chili powder

**Instructions:**

1. **Cook Rice:** Prepare 2 cups of rice according to package instructions. Stir in juice of 1 lime and chopped cilantro after cooking.
2. **Season & Cook Chicken:** Season chicken breasts with cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook on a grill pan or in a skillet until cooked through. Slice or shred chicken.
3. **Assemble Bowls:** Layer rice, black beans, chicken, salsa, avocado slices, and a dollop of Greek yogurt or sour cream (optional) in bowls.

**Recipe 3: Cheesy Chicken & Broccoli Rice Casserole**

* **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
* **Additional Ingredients:** Cheddar cheese, cream of mushroom soup, bread crumbs

**Instructions:**

1. **Preheat Oven:** to 350°F (175°C).
2. **Cook Rice & Broccoli:** Prepare 2 cups of rice according to package instructions. Steam broccoli until tender-crisp.
3. **Combine Ingredients:** In a large bowl, mix cooked rice, broccoli, shredded cooked chicken, cream of mushroom soup, and 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese.
4. **Transfer & Bake:** Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish. Top with remaining cheese and breadcrumbs. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.

**Recipe 4: Protein-Packed Breakfast Burritos**

* **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
* **Additional Ingredients:** Whole wheat tortillas, cheese (cheddar or Monterey Jack), hot sauce (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. **Scramble Eggs:** Whisk 10 eggs with a splash of milk, salt, and pepper. Scramble in a pan with a little olive oil until cooked through.
2. **Warm Tortillas:** Heat tortillas in a microwave or dry skillet.
3. **Assemble Burritos:** Fill each tortilla with scrambled eggs, shredded cheese, and your favorite hot sauce (optional). Roll tightly and enjoy.

**Recipe 5: Chicken & Veggie Omelette Extravaganza**

* **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
* **Additional Ingredients:** Bell peppers, onion, mushrooms (optional)

**Instructions:**

1. **Prep Veggies:** Dice bell peppers, onion, and mushrooms (if using).
2. **Sauté Veggies:** Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté veggies until softened.
3. **Whisk Eggs:** In a bowl, whisk together 10 eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. 
4. **Cook Omelettes:** Pour half the egg mixture into the pan with the veggies. Cook until the bottom is set, then add shredded cooked chicken to one half. Fold the omelette over and cook for another minute. Repeat with remaining egg mixture and filling. 
5. **Serve:** Top with salsa or hot sauce (optional) and a side of your favorite fruit. 

**Important Notes:**

* These recipes are starting points, feel free to adjust seasonings and ingredients to your liking.
* Always cook chicken to an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) to ensure it's cooked through.
* Consider adding a side salad or extra vegetables to your meals for added nutrients and fiber.
