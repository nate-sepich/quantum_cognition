## 5 High-Protein, High-Carb Recipes (Under 30-Min Prep)

Here are 5 recipes using your inventory and aiming for your macro goals, with additional ingredients for variety:

**Recipe 1: Teriyaki Chicken & Veggie Rice Bowl**

* **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
* **Ingredients:**
    * 1 Chicken Breast (170g)
    * 1 cup cooked Brown Rice (195g)
    * 1 cup Broccoli florets (91g)
    * 1 tbsp Olive Oil (14g)
    * 2 tbsp Teriyaki Sauce (40g) 
    * 1/4 cup chopped Pineapple (60g)
    * 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds (9g)

* **Instructions:**
    1. Cook rice according to package directions.
    2. Cut chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
    3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    4. Cook chicken until browned and cooked through.
    5. Add broccoli and pineapple to the skillet.
    6. Pour teriyaki sauce over chicken and vegetables, stirring to coat.
    7. Serve chicken and vegetables over rice. 
    8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. 

**Recipe 2: Salmon & Spinach Frittata with Quinoa Salad**

* **Prep Time:** 25 minutes
* **Ingredients:**
    * 1 Salmon fillet (150g)
    * 1 cup cooked Quinoa (185g)
    * 1 cup Spinach (30g)
    * 4 Eggs 
    * 1/4 cup Greek Yogurt (60g)
    * 1/4 cup chopped Red Onion (40g)
    * 1 tbsp Lemon Juice (15g)
    * Salt and pepper to taste

* **Instructions:**
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Cook quinoa according to package directions.
    3. Whisk eggs, Greek yogurt, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
    4. Heat a little olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
    5. Sauté red onion until softened. 
    6. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
    7. Pour egg mixture into the skillet.
    8. Place salmon fillet on top of the egg mixture.
    9. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the frittata is set.
    10. While the frittata bakes, toss cooked quinoa with lemon juice.
    11. Serve frittata with quinoa salad. 

**Recipe 3: Chicken & Almond Butter Stir-Fry**

* **Prep Time:** 20 minutes
* **Ingredients:**
    * 1 Chicken Breast (170g)
    * 1 cup Broccoli florets (91g)
    * 1/2 cup chopped Bell Pepper (75g)
    * 1 tbsp Olive Oil (14g)
    * 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (38g)
    * 1 tbsp Honey (21g)
    * 1 tbsp Almond Butter (32g)
    * 1 cup cooked Brown Rice (195g)

* **Instructions:**
    1. Cook rice according to package directions.
    2. Cut chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
    3. Heat olive oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. 
    4. Stir-fry chicken until browned and cooked through.
    5. Add broccoli and bell pepper to the wok.
    6. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, and almond butter. 
    7. Pour sauce over chicken and vegetables, stirring constantly until thickened.
    8. Serve chicken and vegetable stir-fry over rice. 

**Recipe 4: Greek Yogurt Bowl with Berries & Nuts**

* **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
* **Ingredients:**
    * 1 cup Greek Yogurt (245g)
    * 1/2 cup mixed Berries (80g)
    * 1/4 cup Almonds (30g)
    * 1/4 cup Rolled Oats (30g) 
    * 1 Banana, sliced (118g)
    * 1 tbsp Honey (21g) (optional)

* **Instructions:**
    1. In a bowl, combine Greek yogurt, berries, almonds, rolled oats, and banana slices.
    2. Drizzle with honey, if desired. 

**Recipe 5: Egg & Veggie Scramble with Whole Wheat Toast**

* **Prep Time:** 15 minutes
* **Ingredients:**
    * 3 Eggs 
    * 1/2 cup chopped Spinach (15g)
    * 1/4 cup chopped Mushrooms (30g)
    * 1/4 cup chopped Tomatoes (35g)
    * 1 tbsp Olive Oil (14g)
    * 2 slices Whole Wheat Toast (60g)

* **Instructions:**
    1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    2. Sauté mushrooms until softened.
    3. Add spinach and tomatoes, cook until spinach is wilted. 
    4. Whisk eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
    5. Pour egg mixture into the skillet with vegetables.
    6. Cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set.
    7. Serve egg scramble with whole wheat toast.
